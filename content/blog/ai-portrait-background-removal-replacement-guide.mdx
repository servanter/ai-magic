## How AI Background Removal Works

Modern AI background removal uses **semantic segmentation** and **deep learning models** trained on millions of portrait images. The process:

1. **Detection**: AI identifies the subject (person) in the image
2. **Edge Detection**: Analyzes boundaries between subject and background
3. **Refinement**: Handles fine details like hair strands and transparent objects
4. **Removal**: Separates subject from background, creating a transparent layer

Popular models include **U²-Net**, **DeepLabV3**, and **MODNet** — all optimized for fast, accurate portrait segmentation.

## Professional Tips for Perfect Results

### 1. **Photo Quality Matters**

For best AI accuracy:
- Use **high-resolution images** (at least 1000px on longest side)
- Ensure **good lighting** on subject
- Avoid **overexposed** or **underexposed** areas
- Minimize **motion blur**

### 2. **Hair & Fine Details**

AI struggles with:
- **Flyaway hair**: Use photos with controlled hair
- **Transparent objects**: Glasses, veils — may need manual refinement
- **Similar colors**: Subject clothing matching background

**Solution**: If edges look rough, use the "Refine Edge" tool to adjust softness and contrast.

### 3. **Background Replacement Best Practices**

When adding new backgrounds:

- **Match lighting direction**: If subject is lit from left, use background with similar lighting
- **Color harmony**: Choose backgrounds that complement subject's clothing/skin tone
- **Resolution**: New background should match or exceed subject resolution
- **Perspective**: Avoid extreme angles that create unnatural depth

### 4. **E-commerce & Professional Use**

For product photos and professional headshots:

- **White background**: Standard for e-commerce (Amazon, eBay)
- **Consistent style**: Use same background across all product images
- **Shadow retention**: Keep natural shadows for realistic grounding
- **Format**: Use **PNG** for transparency, **JPG** for solid backgrounds
