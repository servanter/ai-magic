## Step-by-Step AI Product Photography Workflow

### Phase 1: Capture Source Photos (15-30 minutes per session)

**Minimal Equipment Needed**:
- Smartphone camera (iPhone 12 or later, Samsung Galaxy S21 or similar)
- Simple white backdrop (poster board, sheet, or paper roll)
- Natural window light or basic LED lighting ($20-$50)
- Tripod or stable surface (optional but helpful)

**Shooting Best Practices**:

1. **Lighting Setup**:
   - Position product near large window for soft natural light
   - Use diffused light to minimize harsh shadows
   - Shoot during overcast days or morning/afternoon for even lighting
   - Alternative: Use two LED panels at 45-degree angles

2. **Background Preparation**:
   - Use any clean, neutral background (white, gray, or solid color)
   - AI will remove background, so perfection not required
   - Ensure background contrasts somewhat with product edges

3. **Product Positioning**:
   - Center product in frame with some space around edges
   - Shoot from multiple angles (front, side, 45-degree, detail shots)
   - Maintain consistent distance and height for product series
   - Capture any important details, textures, or features

4. **Camera Settings** (if using DSLR/mirrorless):
   - ISO: 100-400 for clean images
   - Aperture: f/8-f/11 for sharp focus throughout product
   - White balance: Daylight or custom for accurate colors
   - Format: RAW for maximum editing flexibility

**Pro Tip**: Take 3-5 photos of each product from slightly different exposures. AI tools can select or merge the best qualities from multiple source images.

### Phase 2: Background Removal with AI (2-5 minutes per image)

**Using AImage for Background Removal**:

1. **Upload Product Photo**:
   - Navigate to AImage Background Removal tool
   - Upload individual image or batch upload multiple products
   - AI processes automatically within seconds

2. **Review Edge Quality**:
   - Zoom in to check edge accuracy around product
   - AI handles most cases perfectly, including complex edges
   - For tricky areas (very fine details, transparent elements), use manual refinement brush

3. **Select Background Option**:
   - Pure white (standard for most marketplaces like Amazon, eBay)
   - Transparent PNG (for website use with custom backgrounds)
   - Custom color matching your brand palette
   - Lifestyle background from template library

4. **Adjust and Export**:
   - Fine-tune any edge imperfections with precision tools
   - Generate shadows if desired (see next section)
   - Export in required format (JPG for white, PNG for transparent)
   - Download in marketplace-required dimensions

**Batch Processing for Multiple Products**:
- Upload 10-50 images simultaneously
- AI processes all automatically with consistent settings
- Review and approve batch results
- Export entire set in minutes rather than hours

### Phase 3: Enhancement and Polish (3-7 minutes per image)

**Color and Lighting Optimization**:

1. **Automatic Enhancement**:
   - Apply AI auto-enhancement for instant improvement
   - AI adjusts exposure, contrast, saturation for optimal appearance
   - Maintains color accuracy while making images pop

2. **Color Accuracy Check**:
   - Compare edited image to actual product or reference photo
   - Adjust color temperature if slight casting remains
   - Ensure consistency across product variations (different colors of same item)

3. **Detail Enhancement**:
   - Apply subtle sharpening to emphasize product texture and details
   - Use noise reduction if source photo was taken in lower light
   - Enhance contrast slightly to add dimension

**Shadow Generation for Realism**:

1. **Assess Shadow Need**:
   - Flat products on white may not need shadows
   - 3D products benefit from drop or cast shadows
   - Reflective products look better with subtle reflection shadows

2. **Apply AI Shadow**:
   - Select shadow type (drop shadow, cast shadow, reflection)
   - AI generates realistic shadow matching product shape
   - Adjust shadow opacity, distance, and blur to taste

3. **Shadow Refinement**:
   - Ensure shadow direction is consistent across all products
   - Match shadow intensity to your brand style
   - Keep shadows subtle—less is more for professional look

### Phase 4: Marketplace Optimization (5 minutes per listing)

**Size and Format Requirements**:

Different marketplaces have specific image requirements:

**Amazon**:
- Minimum: 1000px on longest side (enables zoom feature)
- Recommended: 2000px for high-quality zoom
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Background: Pure white (RGB 255, 255, 255) for main image
- File size: Under 10MB

**eBay**:
- Minimum: 500px on longest side
- Recommended: 1600px for SuperSize feature
- Format: JPG, PNG, GIF
- No strict background requirements

**Etsy**:
- Minimum: 2000px on shortest side
- Square format recommended (1:1 ratio)
- Format: JPG, PNG, GIF
- File size: Under 10MB

**Shopify/Own Website**:
- Recommended: 2048px or larger for Retina displays
- Format: JPG for photos, PNG for graphics/transparency
- Progressive JPG for faster loading
- Optimize file size (100-300KB ideal)

**Using AI for Marketplace Compliance**:
- Batch resize to meet each marketplace's specifications
- Automatically generate required aspect ratios
- Ensure pure white backgrounds meet marketplace standards
- Compress files while maintaining quality

### Phase 5: Lifestyle and Contextual Variations (Optional, 10 minutes)

**Creating Lifestyle Images with AI**:

Beyond clean product-on-white images, lifestyle photos show products in use:

**AI Background Replacement**:
- Upload product photo with removed background
- Select from library of lifestyle settings (kitchen, bedroom, office, outdoor)
- AI composites product naturally into scene
- Adjust lighting and shadows to match environment

**Use Cases for Lifestyle Images**:
- Secondary product images on listings (2nd, 3rd image after main white background)
- Social media marketing and ads
- Email marketing campaigns
- Website category or collection headers

**Creating Variations**:
- Holiday/seasonal backgrounds (Christmas, summer, back-to-school)
- Demographic-targeted settings (family home, single apartment, office)
- Use-case scenarios (product being used for intended purpose)

**AI Tools for Background Replacement**:
- PhotoRoom: Extensive lifestyle background library
- Canva: Large template collection with easy customization
- AImage: Curated backgrounds optimized for e-commerce

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Mistake 1: Over-Editing and Unrealistic Representation

**The Problem**: Making products look too perfect or different from reality

**Consequences**:
- Customer disappointment upon receiving product
- Higher return rates and negative reviews
- Damaged reputation and trust

**AI Tools Can Enable This**:
- Excessive skin smoothing on fabric textures
- Unrealistic color enhancement
- Adding details or features that don't exist

**Best Practice**:
- Use AI to enhance, not misrepresent
- Aim for "best possible version of reality" not fantasy
- Test with real customer feedback
- Always compare edited image to actual product side-by-side

### Mistake 2: Inconsistent Styling Across Catalog

**The Problem**: Each product edited differently creates unprofessional appearance

**Consequences**:
- Catalog looks disorganized and amateur
- Reduces brand perception and trust
- Customers uncertain about quality consistency

**How It Happens with AI**:
- Using different tools or settings for different product batches
- Not establishing standard workflow
- Forgetting to apply brand style guide

**Best Practice**:
- Create documented editing workflow with specific settings
- Use AI presets or templates for consistency
- Process similar products in same batch with identical settings
- Regular catalog audits to identify and fix inconsistencies

### Mistake 3: Ignoring Marketplace Requirements

**The Problem**: Images don't meet technical specifications and get rejected

**Consequences**:
- Listings disabled or deprioritized in search results
- Loss of sales during time required for corrections
- Extra work re-editing non-compliant images

**Common Compliance Issues**:
- Amazon main images without pure white backgrounds
- Insufficient resolution for zoom features
- Incorrect aspect ratios or dimensions
- File sizes too large or too small

**Best Practice**:
- Research specific requirements for each sales channel before editing
- Configure AI tools with marketplace-specific presets
- Automated compliance checking before upload
- Keep reference checklist for each platform

### Mistake 4: Neglecting Mobile Viewing Experience

**The Problem**: Images look great on desktop but fail on mobile devices

**Consequences**:
- 70%+ of e-commerce traffic is mobile—poor mobile images directly hurt sales
- Products too small or details unclear on phone screens
- Slow loading times from oversized files

**How It Happens**:
- Editing and reviewing only on desktop monitors
- Not optimizing file sizes for mobile networks
- Products too small within frame for mobile viewing

**Best Practice**:
- Always preview edited images on mobile device before publishing
- Ensure products fill adequate portion of frame (85%+ for main images)
- Use AI compression to optimize file sizes (under 200KB ideal)
- Test loading speed on mobile connection

### Mistake 5: Skipping A/B Testing

**The Problem**: Assuming one style works best without data validation

**Consequences**:
- Missing opportunities for higher conversion rates
- Continuing with suboptimal image strategies
- Not understanding what resonates with your specific customers

**Why It's Easy to Skip**:
- Takes time and effort to test variations
- False confidence that "professional-looking" automatically equals "best-converting"
- Not tracking image-specific performance data

**Best Practice**:
- Test different background styles (pure white vs lifestyle vs colored)
- Test different angles and compositions
- Test with and without shadows
- Track conversion rates by image style and iterate
- Use AI to quickly generate variations for testing
